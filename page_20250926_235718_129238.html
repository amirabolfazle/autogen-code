About

This is a compact, single-file portfolio crafted to feel kinetic and futuristic while keeping everything self-contained. It uses a neon palette, subtle glow, and a zoom-on-hover interaction to give depth without external dependencies.

You can easily customize the content, add more sections, or swap in your own projects. The design prioritizes readability and fast loading by avoiding external assets.
