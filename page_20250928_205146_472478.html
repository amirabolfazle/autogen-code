Neon Intro

Glow-driven visuals with layered gradients and soft blur effects. Smooth animations bring focus to elements without overwhelming the user.

Slide-up Effects

Cards animate with a subtle slide-up and fade when they enter the viewport. The effect is implemented with modern Intersection Observer patterns.

Responsive Layout

The grid adapts from three columns down to a single column on small screens, ensuring a friendly mobile experience.

Accessible

Semantic HTML and focusable controls maintain keyboard and assistive technology support. Color contrast keeps the neon theme readable.

Performance

Animations are GPU-friendly transforms and opacity changes. Background accents are subtle and tuned for low overhead.

Customize

Colors, glow intensity, and animation timing can be tweaked via CSS variables at the top of the document for quick adjustments.
