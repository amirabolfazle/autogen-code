Welcome

Vibrant Identity

Colors, glow and soft glass surfaces combine to create a neon identity. Each block slides up as it enters the viewport, adding a lively, dynamic feel.

Interactions

Subtle Motion

Animations are intentionally smooth and light to keep the focus on content while enhancing perceived speed and polish.

Responsive

Layout Adapts

Grid-based layout collapses elegantly for narrow viewports. Typography scales and spacing adjusts to keep things readable and balanced.

Accessibility

High Contrast Options

Neon colors sit on a dark backdrop for clear contrast. UI elements include adequate hit areas and legible text sizes for most devices.

Performance

GPU-Accelerated Motion

Transforms and opacity are used for animations to keep things smooth even on lower-powered devices. IntersectionObserver triggers animations only when needed.

Customization

Easy to Theme

Root variables hold core colors and radii. Swap a few values or gradients and the whole vibe shifts instantly. Try a cooler or warmer palette.
